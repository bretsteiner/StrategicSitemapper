The methodology

Clarity first.
Build the right thing, faster — and cheaper.

Strategic Sitemapper grew out of years of digital stakeholder management. The insight was simple: when every person in the room is looking at the same picture, the entire project changes.

Expensive guesswork is the default mode for most transformation programmes. Teams align to different versions of reality, decisions get made without full system visibility, and rework becomes the biggest hidden cost on the budget. Bret Steiner developed Strategic Sitemapper as a direct response to this pattern — a tool that makes the shared picture compulsory, not aspirational.
